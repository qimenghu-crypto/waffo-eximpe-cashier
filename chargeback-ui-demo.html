Decision Guidance
This section helps merchants understand when to defend and when to accept before taking a high-impact action.

When to defend

  • You have valid evidence showing the transaction was authorized.
  • You can provide proof of delivery, service fulfillment, or subscription consent.
  • You can explain the case clearly before the deadline.

When to accept

  • You do not have sufficient evidence.
  • You agree not to dispute the case further.
  • You understand the chargeback amount and fee impact.
Evidence Submission
Upload clear supporting evidence and summarize your position for the reviewer.

Recommended materials

  • Proof of fulfillment / delivery
  • Customer authorization proof
  • Subscription agreement or service terms
  • Communication history with customer
  • Invoice or order confirmation

Submission notes

  • Submit before the deadline.
  • Incomplete evidence may reduce review quality.
  • Use Message to summarize why the case should be defended.
